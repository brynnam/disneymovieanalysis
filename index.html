Inclusive Ink

Exploring LGBTQ+ Visibility in Youth Cartoon Universes

Introduction

In the dynamic landscape of youth cartoons, the representation of LGBTQ characters has emerged as a topic of increasing importance and scrutiny. As society's understanding and acceptance of diverse identities evolve, so does the portrayal of LGBTQ characters in animated media. Using a detailed dataset, I hope to dive into the evolution of LGBTQ representation in youth cartoons over time, examining potential trends and patterns in their portrayal across various dimensions such as studios, platforms, and character roles. By exploring these features, I aim to answer and shed light on exactly how much progress has been made in terms of depicting LGBTQ identities within the realm of animated entertainment.
